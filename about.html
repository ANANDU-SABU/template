kerala foodie is new startup resturant, the main focusing is delievering good food and good service to their customer in a afforable rate ,about the food it is Indulge in the rich culinary tapestry of Kerala, a paradise for food enthusiasts. Kerala's cuisine is a harmonious blend of aromatic spices, coconut-infused flavors, and fresh local produce, celebrated for its diverse range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian delicacies. From the iconic appam and stew to the fiery fish curry, the delectable banana leaf meals, and the mouthwatering seafood specialties, every dish tells a story deeply rooted in tradition. Experience the unique taste of Kerala through its spicy curries, fluffy rice preparations, and delectable snacks like banana chips. A journey through Kerala's foods is a journey through its culture and heritage, promising a savory adventure that tantalizes the taste buds and nourishes the soul.
